[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Kaale surme de vich lukkya ae nasha, fatal attraction
Sachi marda haan wekh ke main teri adaa, chain reaction
Akhiyan di agg vich balda haan roz, pure burning fire
Bullan te tera naa labhda haan khoz, my only desire

[Hook 2]
Teri ik niki jehi jhalak de piche ghumda, breaking the rules
Har thaan te bas tainu hi main labhda, playing the fools
Zulfan di ghani chhanv thalle aake rukda, hiding the pain
Saanh vi mere hun wekh ke tainu sukkda, going insane

[Verse 1]
Kach de glass vich dulleya ae mera raatan da khwab, toxic potion
Main sab kujh bhul gya haan wekh ke tera shabab, lost in motion
Tere aunde hi chha gyi ae ik wakhri jehi garmi, heavy heat
Dil mera zoraan naal dhardakda ae khake narmi, skipping the beat
Ik pase duniya te duje pase tuhi mere khyalan ch, on my mind
Aisa jaadu tu kitta ae main fass gya sawalan ch, leaving behind
Main kalla khada reh gya haan inna tarya di roshni ch, under the moon
Teri mithi jehi khushbu aundi ae hawa di taazgi ch, floral bloom

[Pre-Chorus]
Taaryan di roshni ch karda haan tera deedar, stealing your glance
Main tere hor nede auna chahva is vaar, taking a chance
Tainu ghutt ke seene naal launa chahva main ajj, holding you tight
Tu ban ja meri rani chadd ke eh saara jagg, into the night

[Chorus]
Oh tera nasha chadheya ae mere sir te, taking over me
Main tera hi ban ke rehna haan har ishaare te, for eternity
Nain tere kaale lutt lende ne meri jaan, casting a spell
Duniya di mainu koi vi hosh nahi ae meri shaan, under your shell
Oh mera deewanapan vadhda hi janda ae roz, reaching the sky
Main sacha ikrar karda haan labhda eh khoz, I will never lie
Bulliyaan da haasa tera karda ae mainu beqabu, keeping me alive
Tu hi meri zind jaan bas chalda ae tera jaadu, reason I survive

[Verse 2]
Kach de sheeshe cho takkda haan dhundli hawa, late night rides
Hawa de vich udd de haan chad ke parwah, shifting the tides
Takk ke tainu engine di ghurrahat vadhdi, entire world
Teri meri yaari di agg seene vich machdi, beautiful girl
Takaan main dashboard di roshni ch tera chehra, breathing the air
Tuhi aen asmaan te tuhi mera savera, without a care
Sadak de kande rokaan main ghumde pahiye, blocking the way
Dil di sachi gal aaj tenu aapan kahiye, what I wanna say
